College on Mental Health

For students, college is a time of liberation, self-discovery, new opportunities, work, study and play. However, there are times when balancing the demands of college can be challenging, and take a toll on one’s mental health. In fact, roughly 73% of students claim that they experienced some sort of mental health crisis during their years spent in college. 

Food on Mental Health

One way to combat potential mental health pitfalls is to eat healthy, and have a well balanced diet. Did you know that a proper diet can help us think clearly,  feel more alert, improve concentration, and attention span. On the contrary, a poor diet can aggravate, and may even lead to, stress and depression.
